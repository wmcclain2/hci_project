About Us

Hello and welcome to Jade Palace! We are a business resort located in PCB Florida that is dedicated to providing an experience that you will never forget. Jade Palace is not your ordinary hotel or resort, in fact we go much further. We are pleased to announce that we are the first resort in the world that is designed solely for party goers. What this means is that our priority is to ensure that our visitors get the ultimate party experience. Some might even say we are a "club on the beach that you can sleep at".

Goal and Business Objective

Our goal and business objective is simple. We want to provide patrons with the best party experience they never had before and will never forget. We want to provide a fun, friendly and safe environment in which partiers can come and have the time of their life. We want visitors to forget about the worry of everyday life and just have fun.
